Facilities At Transit Men's Hostel

A large sized LED TV together with a set up box is available for boarders loaded with entertainment channel, news channel , sports etc

Water Cooler

Chilled and Purified water available at both floors ...

RO Water Purifier

Electrical RO Water Purifier is on both the floors of the hostel

Laundry Facility - Washing Machine

Washing Machine is available for all the boarders of the hostel and hostel staff throughout the day ...

Indoor Table Tennis & Carrom

Games required kits are available for both the games ...

Mess

Veg and Non-Veg Mess is available where food is served as per the menu prepared by the hostel mess committee & boarders. Both Hostel boarders and staff can avail the facility ...

Common Room is the place where boarders can relax and spend their free time with their friends playing games like carrom , table tennis or just discussing about their day with their friends ...

Tiled & Clean Floor

The hostel is well maintained and cleaned by the hostel cleaning staff regularly.

Cycle Stand
